Welcome to our image gallery. Here you can find a collection of random images that demonstrate how to work with image embedding in HTML. Each image has been sourced from Lorem Picsum, offering a random selection of beautiful and interesting visuals that you can use to practice linking images in your web pages.

The gallery is an example of how you can use the "tag in HTML to display images on your website. We've provided different image sizes and examples of both internal and external images, which is essential for building dynamic and visually engaging websites.

Feel free to explore the images, and don't forget to try embedding your own images on your websites. Understanding how to use relative and absolute paths for images is a crucial skill when developing your web pages. Try experimenting with different images and layouts to enhance the look and feel of your website.
